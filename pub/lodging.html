    • Places to Stay

    If you are so inclined, bring a tent and camp on the property Saturday night! We have plenty of space for it. Come by the river house and set up before the ceremony.

    Otherwise, book a room in Reedville, a five minute drive from the River house. Here are our favorites.

    Fleeton Fields
    The Gables
    Grandview Bed and Breakfast

    Kilmarnock also has some nice accommodations a fifteen minute drive from the River house.

    The Hope and Glory Inn
    The Kilmarnock Inn
    Back Inn Time
    Holiday Inn Express

    When you call to book, tell them you are with us. Many of the hotels in the area are holding rooms for our guests, and have agreed not to rent them to anyone else until about mid-April. Don't wait til the last minute!
