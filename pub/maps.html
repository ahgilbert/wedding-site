Maps and Directions

The Northern Neck gets real dark at night, and cell reception isn't what it is in most parts of the world, so make sure you have a good idea of where you are going before you start out.

Ceremony to Reception


View Larger Map

Drive straight out of the park on Belle Isle Road. Turn Right onto S. River Road (3 miles). Turn left onto White Chapel Road (3 miles). Turn right onto Mary Ball Road (5 miles). Turn left onto Pinckardsville Road (1.8 miles). Continue onto Crawford's Corner. Turn right onto Mount Olive Road (1.7 miles). Turn left onto Jesse Dupont Highway (5 miles). Turn right onto Old Glebe Point Road (.5 miles). Turn right onto Blackwells Wharf Road. The drive way is a half a mile down on the right. 652 Blackwells Wharf Road, Burgess, VA

Reception to Reedville


View Larger Map

Turn left out of the driveway onto Blackwells Wharf Road. Turn right onto Old Glebe Point Road (.2 miles). Take the second left towards Jessie Dupont. Turn right onto Jessie Dupont (1 mile). Turn right onto Northcumberland Highway which will turn into main street Reedville after a five mile drive.
